About the Book

A practical framework for disciplined property investing

The Spanish Wealth Model presents a clear system for evaluating residential investment opportunities with consistent assumptions and practical decision logic.

The focus is not on speculation, hype, or quick wins. It is on cashflow quality, financing structure, and the long-term mechanics of building wealth through property.

Inspired by real-world investing in Spain, the approach stays grounded in practical deal analysis, sustainable rental strategies, and repeatable portfolio thinking.
